Our story

Built by a father,
inspired by his daughter

"Every child deserves a voice, even if it sounds different."

Vani was created for my daughter Shavya, who is autistic and experiences communication challenges in everyday life.

As a parent, I saw how hard it could be when she wanted to express a need, a feeling, or a thought — but didn't always have the words at the right moment. We tried different tools, but many felt complicated, expensive, or hard to personalize for real family life. So I decided to build something ourselves.

I'm a software engineer, but this app didn't start as a business idea. It started at home — asking for food, expressing emotions, choosing activities, connecting with family. Every feature comes from real experiences raising a child with autism and ADHD.

The goal isn't only to help children communicate, but to help parents feel understood, hopeful, and supported on their journey.

— Pardeep Father of Shavya
